Macaroni and Cheese

Description :

Macaroni and cheese is a pasta dish of macaroni covered in cheese sauce, most commonly cheddar sauce.

Ingredients :

  • Macaroni
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps :

  1. Boil the macaroni.
  2. While we wait for the macaroni, melt the butter in a saucepan.
  3. Add milk, flour, salt and cheese to the saucepan.
  4. Lastly, add the boiled macaroni.
